## Opening Narrative (30 seconds)

Every year, millions of people — seniors, caregivers, and busy professionals — lose connection not because they don’t care, but because life overwhelms memory.
Memories scatter. Relationships fade. Important moments slip away.

Revivo is built to solve this deeply human problem.

## The Problem

People today face two simultaneous challenges:

We forget important life details — conversations, moments, commitments, anniversaries, people.

We drift away from loved ones — relationships weaken not from intention, but from overload.

For seniors and early cognitive decline, this loss is even more profound: it impacts orientation, confidence, and emotional wellbeing.

There is no holistic system today that helps people remember, reconnect, and revive their human relationships.

## The Solution: Revivo

Revivo is a human-centered memory OS that organizes your life and strengthens your relationships.

It automatically captures moments from chats, emails, places, diary entries, voice notes, and daily interactions — then uses lightweight, contextual AI to:

help you remember important details

nudge you to reconnect with people

weave your memories into a coherent life story

support orientation and emotional continuity for seniors

## Revivo isn’t a chatbot.
It’s a connection companion.

A tool that brings people closer to other people.

## Why Now

Revivo sits at the intersection of health, wellbeing, productivity, and emotional intelligence.
